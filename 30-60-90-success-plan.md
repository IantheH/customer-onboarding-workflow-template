# First 30 Days: Setup, Clarity & First Value

## Focus

The first 30 days should help the customer understand the platform, complete core setup, and reach an initial meaningful outcome.

## Goals

- Confirm business goals and success criteria
- Identify stakeholders, admins, and daily users
- Map current workflows
- Configure core platform settings
- Deliver initial training
- Achieve first-value milestone

## Key Activities

- Kickoff call completed
- User access configured
- Core workflows mapped
- CRM fields, pipelines, templates, or automations configured
- Integrations reviewed or connected
- Initial training delivered
- Support expectations clarified
- First-value milestone achieved

## 30-Day Checkpoint Questions

- Has the customer completed initial setup?
- Are users clear on how to use the platform?
- Has the customer achieved the first-value milestone?
- Are there any blockers slowing adoption?
- What additional training or documentation is needed?

---

# First 60 Days: Adoption & Workflow Consistency

## Focus

The next 30 days should help the customer build consistent usage habits, resolve blockers, and deepen adoption.

## Goals

- Review usage patterns
- Resolve support issues and workflow gaps
- Support additional users or teams
- Improve automations and integrations
- Document repeatable processes
- Encourage consistent product usage

## Key Activities

- Adoption check-in completed
- Usage patterns reviewed
- Common questions documented
- Workflow gaps identified
- Automations refined
- Additional training provided
- Support issues reviewed
- Customer feedback captured

## 60-Day Checkpoint Questions

- Are users logging in and completing key workflows?
- Which features are being adopted?
- Which features are underused?
- Are workflows becoming easier or more consistent?
- What friction still exists?
- Are stakeholders engaged?

---

# First 90 Days: Outcomes & Long-Term Value

## Focus

By 90 days, the customer should be moving from setup and adoption toward measurable business value and long-term success planning.

## Goals

- Review progress against original goals
- Identify adoption gaps
- Confirm business outcomes
- Recommend next workflows or features
- Align on ongoing success plan
- Prepare for account review or QBR

## Key Activities

- 90-day success review completed
- Business goals revisited
- Adoption gaps documented
- Customer health reviewed
- Next-step recommendations provided
- Long-term support rhythm confirmed
- Expansion or optimization opportunities noted, if relevant

## 90-Day Checkpoint Questions

- What outcomes has the customer achieved?
- What value has the customer experienced so far?
- What workflows still need improvement?
- Are there risks to retention or long-term adoption?
- What should the customer focus on next?
- What support cadence makes sense going forward?
